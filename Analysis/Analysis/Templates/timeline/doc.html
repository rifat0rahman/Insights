'INSIGHTS' helps you to see your ugly data.

To visualize your data:

  • At fisrt you need to upload a csv file (we are taking only csv file for now)
  • You can customize the file length (number of row)
  • Have a look at the DataFrame.
  • Click `Get Charts` to go to the charts page
  • Select the columns you want to visualize.You can select multiple columns and compare them together.
  • We just bring the numeric columns which could be visualized
  • We don't save your data
    UPLOAD A CSV FILE

You can customize the row of your data (red block) . Then click `choose file` to select a csv file from your device.Then click `submit` (yellow block). Now you can see a DataFrame created based on your csv file.

    GET CHARTS

After exploring the DataFrame.Click `Get Charts` to go to the Charts page

    SELECT COLUMNS

If you notice there are missing some of the columns.That only because of that are not numeric data.Only numeric data can build a chart.

Now click on the column buttons . you can select multiple.

Here I have selected 'math score and 'reading score' . You can easily delete the columns by just clicking that.Now to see the charts you need have a sleep(cringe). click the `show charts` button to see the beatiful charts.
